4. Review Pipeline (Human Gate)

Every piece of content passes through Bene before publishing. No exceptions. No auto-publish.

Review Flow
Step 1
Generate Preview — Blog: PDF + audio MP3. Carousel: PDF of 5 slides. Both generated via pandoc/enscript/ps2pdf.
Hugo
Step 2
Send to Bene — Telegram message with PDF attachment and inline ✅/🔄 buttons. Blog posts also include audio voice message.
Hugo → Bene
Step 3
Bene Decides — Tap ✅ to approve (triggers publish script). Tap 🔄 to request revision (resets 12-hour reminder clock).
Bene
Step 4a
On Approve — Blog: flip draft:false, commit, push → Netlify auto-deploy. Carousel: post to LinkedIn + Instagram APIs.
Auto
Step 4b
On Revise — Status → revision_requested. Bene provides feedback. Gary revises. Resubmits through same loop.
Gary
Step 5
Reminder Loop — Heartbeat checks pending items every 12 hours. Sends Telegram reminder if unreviewed. No silent expiry.
Hugo
🛡️ Golden Rules
  • Content delivered as PDF via Telegram. Never raw text.
  • Blog posts get audio version (when voice-briefing available).
  • Approval triggers publish script automatically.
  • Do NOT push draft:false until approved.
  • No text-only LinkedIn posts — carousel only.
  • Max 3 items in review queue at once.

7. Key Design Decisions

🚫
No Auto-Publish
Human gate on everything
Every piece of content requires Bene's explicit approval before publishing. Scripts run only after ✅ button tap. No exceptions.
🎯
Carousel-Only Social
No text-only posts
All LinkedIn and Instagram content is 5-slide carousels. Quote cards and text posts deprecated. Higher engagement, better brand consistency.
🔗
Blog as Anchor
Everything derives from it
Weekly blog article is the core asset. Carousels, newsletter, and YouTube expand angles from it. Atoms extracted post-publish feed the social pipeline.
🤖
Agentic by Default
Human + AI hybrid
Gary (AI) produces drafts. Bene (human) approves. Hugo (AI) handles routing and reminders. Scout runs autonomously but Bene decides what to build.
📊
Measure Before Swap
Infrastructure decision rule
No model/tool/framework swaps without evidence of specific failure. "Current works but better is available" → rejected automatically. Codified in AGENTS.md.
🔄
Compounding Assets
Atoms DB + publish log
Every published piece generates reusable atoms. Every action is logged. The system gets smarter over time through accumulated intelligence, not just model upgrades.
