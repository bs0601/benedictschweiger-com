Build the system that lets you charge more — and keep the margin.

The Margin Method is a five-phase loop (Listen, Reach, Capture, Convert, Measure) that turns customer truth into higher value per customer. AI agents are how you run it without a team you can't afford.

This is for you if you run marketing for an established consumer brand doing roughly €1M to €20M, mostly on your own, and the business keeps growing while your profit barely moves. You suspect the fix is structural. You do not have the time or the team to build it. That is exactly what the Margin Method is for.
